Running visual queries on my name yields fluctuating results year to year. Images and layers decay and slough off or rejuvenate.

One year I am an old me, the same as a young me, one year a self initiated alias on social sites no longer trafficked.

Another I am a Formula One driver, or more specifically the driver and his Formula One car, I am both, operator and instrument.

The current consensus is that I am a character in a Japanese video game series, who is also a vampire. My newly materialized wikipedia page is more interesting than it has ever been, and according to it I am over 2400 years old and my last words as a mortal being were:

“Accept me and I shall grant life everlasting. Though it shall be filled with grief, thou shalt have eternity to wreak vengeance upon thine enemies.”
